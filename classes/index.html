Girl Develop It Columbus teaches classes, builds community, and provides resources for web and software development education for women and men in Columbus, Ohio. Our goal is to provide a low-pressure, friendly and fun learning environment and give students usable tools and expectations in order to achieve their own goals.

We accomplish this by:

  • providing introductory, multi-week courses in programming and HTML/CSS, as well as more advanced topics that are still designed to be welcoming and non-intimidating
  • creating workshops for exploration of topics or audiences beyond our regular courses (for example, working with HTML5 and CSS3 specifically, or a kid/family-oriented workshop)
  • hosting diverse speakers on various coding topics
  • offering direction to the most appropriate external resources so that students can learn in the ways best suited to them
  • establishing good communication between teachers and students
  • focusing our teaching approach so that students feel comfortable asking questions, engaged in the material and empowered to learn difficult concepts
  • highlighting women currently working in the development field as role models

Current Classes

We keep the list of upcoming classes, class registration and other class details at our Meetup group. If a particular class is full, you can join its waitlist in the case that a spot opens up. You can also join our mailing list to receive first notice of new classes.
